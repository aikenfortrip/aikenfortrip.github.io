Here’s to hoping you will be embarking on your best trip ever! Please don’t forget…

Passport

Please make sure your passport is valid for at least 6 months and one day from your RETURN date.

Insurance

To maximize your benefits, please make sure traveller insurance is purchased no more than 10 days of your FIRST deposit of your trip.If you add additional non refundable costs while planing your adventure, please notify me ASAP about the additional costs so your policy always remains valid.

Credit Cards

Please contact all credit card/debit cards you plan to take with you about the details of your trip. It is not much fun to find THE purchase of the trip only to have your credit card denied.Also inform the companies of any layovers you have scheduled (just in case!) Before you leave make copies of the front and back of all the cards you are taking with you and leave one copy behind with a family member or friend.

Vehicles

If renting a car overseas, please obtain an international driver’s license before you leave home.

Currency

In advance, procure local currency. It should be in small clean denominations.
